How it works

Designed for safe TV sign-in and direct playback.

Secure sign-in

Users sign in with Google on a phone or browser and approve Drive read-only access for their own account.

Read-only Drive access

The app requests Drive read-only permission to list and stream media files without modifying or deleting files.

Direct streaming

Video and audio playback streams directly from Google Drive to the TV app, not through our backend servers.

Data use

Your files stay yours.

DriveTV Player uses Google Drive access only to display file metadata, browse folders, and play media selected by the user. We do not sell user data, and the app does not request permission to edit or delete Drive files.
